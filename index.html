A Simple Approach to Personal Finance
Savings, Income, Debts and Expenses

You suck at money management. Well, unless you don't. But if you're like most millenials, you probably do. Personal finance is just one of those things that young people have a hard time mastering.

From our parents to countless books like "Rags To Riches", "Zombie Finance", and "Rich Dad, Poor Dad" - there's a plethora of tools that try to help tame the wily ways of our spending and saving habits. But, they don't often stick.

While this tool won't put money in your pocket, it can help you get a high level understanding of where your money should be going.

You have income and then everything else...Expenses, are the usual suspects - rent, cell phone bill, groceries, etc. Debts are things like credit cards and student loans. Savings represent your monthly contribution to an Emergency Fund, 401k, or Investment account.

Plug in some numbers, gain some perspective and have some fun.
